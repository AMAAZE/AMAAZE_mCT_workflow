The first workflow 00_share_data.py

Welcome to the AMAAZE mCT surfacing flow. 
We are glad you're here. 
Please allow us to ask a few questions before we begin the workflow.

Once you finish answering our questions, we will be able to begin the workflow.

There are 5 required steps and 2 optional steps in this workflow.
01_set_rotation.py is an interactive step where we will examine a representative slice and make some adjustments like rotation and cropping. 
02_segment.py is an automated step. 
03_segment.py is an interactive step where you will verify or amend suggestions we make for segmenting your scan into individual files per specimen.
04_surface.py is an automated step that will create the 3D meshes of your data
Once this step is complete you can do a spot check to see if your meshes turned out.
05_clean_meshes is another automated step that will clean your files.

The optional steps include 06_render_views.py which creates images you can put into publications or on your website and 07_build_contact_sheet.py which creates a contact sheet for quick review. which is especially helpful if you are surfacing scans for someone else and they need a quick way to verify whether or not we found success.
